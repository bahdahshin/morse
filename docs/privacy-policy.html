Privacy Policy for Morse

Last updated: June 25, 2026

Morse is a simple iOS app that turns the screen white and enables the device torch while the screen is being touched. The app is designed to work entirely on your device.

Data Collection

Morse does not collect, store, sell, or share personal information.

Camera Permission

Morse requests camera access only so it can control the rear camera torch. The app does not take photos, record videos, access your photo library, or transmit camera data.

Analytics and Tracking

Morse does not use analytics, advertising, tracking technologies, or third-party SDKs.

Children's Privacy

Morse does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through the app's App Store support link.
